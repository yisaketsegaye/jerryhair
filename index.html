About

A salon suite built around you.

Bellinis Salon Suite + Blow Bar is an intimate studio that focuses on effortless hair, custom color, and polished yet relaxed blowouts. Each service is designed to feel unhurried, thoughtful, and tailored to your lifestyle.

From dimensional color and low-maintenance blondes to smooth blowouts and special occasion styling, every appointment starts with a detailed consultation and a clear plan for your hair goals.

Signature blonding lived-in, dimensional, and soft grow-out.
Luxury blowouts polished, bouncy, and camera-ready.
Extension services length, volume, and fullness with a custom blend.
Quiet, private space one-on-one appointments in a cozy suite.

Your experience

Expect a curated playlist, complimentary beverage, and a calm atmosphere from the moment you walk in. Your appointment includes a thorough consultation, scalp massage, and personalized product recommendations so you can recreate your look at home.

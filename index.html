About

Your neighborhood stylist in Wheaton.

Jerry’s Hair Salon is a private suite inside My Salon Suite at Westfield Wheaton Mall. The space is relaxed, welcoming and focused on giving you a clean, polished style that fits your everyday life.

From simple trims to full color, braids, weave installs and treatments, each appointment includes a consultation so you and your stylist are on the same page before any service begins.

Men’s and women’s cuts Clean, sharp cuts and trims that match your style.
Color and highlights Single process, partial and full highlights with styling.
Braids and weave Braiding, weave and wig installs plus natural hair care.
Treatments and more Relaxers, treatments, perms and eyebrow waxing.

How to find the salon

Jerry’s Hair Salon is located on the lower level of Westfield Wheaton Mall inside My Salon Suite.

Use the north side parking and enter at the lower level. Once inside, follow signs to My Salon Suite and look for Suite 114.
